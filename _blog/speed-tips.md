Small habits add up. Here are three that pair well with ToolsVault:

1. **Press `/`** anywhere on the homepage to jump straight to search.
2. **Favorite often-used tools** with the heart — they collect in the Favorites tab.
3. **Hit `Esc`** to clear a search without reaching for the mouse.

That's it — a few keystrokes shave seconds off every lookup.
